Engagespot adds web push notifications to your Wordpress website. Connect with your visitors even when they are not on your website.

== Description ==
To get started, just enter your Engagespot API credentials. You can find these values in the Website Settings menu from your Engagespot dashboard.

What is Engagespot?

Engagespot is a web push based marketing service that lets you send push notifications from your website directly to your visitors desktop. For this, your visitors should subscribe by clicking the Bell button which will appear on your website. Also, you can send targeted push notifications to double the result of your push marketing campaign and bring your visitors back to your website.

For feedbacks and suggestions, please contact us at support@engagespot.co

== Installation ==
Wordpress : Extract the zip file and just drop the contents in the wp-content/plugins/ directory of your WordPress installation and then activate the Plugin from Plugins page.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= I can’t see the bell icon in some web browsers =
Engagespot is currently supported in Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox only. We plans to add support other browsers in future.

= Can I remove the ‘Powered by Engagespot’ link from my notification panel? =
Yes you can remove that link by upgrading your account. For more details visit our website https://engagespot.co
